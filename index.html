About the Restaurant

Fresh. Local. Refined.

Maison Verde is a chef-driven restaurant focused on seasonal ingredients and refined comfort dishes. Our kitchen blends classic techniques with a modern aesthetic, delivering a menu that evolves with the market.

We offer a 5-course tasting menu, artisanal desserts, and a curated wine list with a focus on natural and biodynamic producers.
